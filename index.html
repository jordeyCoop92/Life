Feeding and Water Changes This Week

F = Fed · W = Water change done

Protocols
Ammonia Protocol

Above 0.5 ppm → 30% change (4.8L) + Aquadene 7–8 drops + Seachem Prime 0.4ml into tank
0.25–0.5 ppm → 20% change (3.2L) + Aquadene 3–4 drops + Seachem Prime 0.4ml into tank
Below 0.25 ppm → No change, can feed small amount
Do not feed if ammonia above 0.5 ppm

Seachem Prime

0.4ml into full 16L tank. Dose directly into tank, not water change volume. Redose every 24hrs if ammonia remains high.

Aquadene

2–3 drops per 3.2L new water only. 7–8 drops for 4.8L change. Add to new water before adding to tank.

Seachem Stability

SHAKE WELL before every use. Day 1: 2ml. Days 2–7: 1ml daily. After day 7: 1ml daily until ammonia AND nitrite = 0 consistently. Once cycled: 1ml monthly.

Foster Clarks Bicarbonate

ONLY when KH below 70 AND pH below 7.0 simultaneously. 1/4 tsp dissolved slowly. NOT baking powder — Foster Clarks orange tin only.

Brief — Tidal and Root

We are Tidal and Root — a brand rooted in the ocean, nature, craft and curiosity. We work at the intersection of conscious travel, beach culture and the natural world. Our audience are people who dive, explore coastlines, choose sustainably and care about beauty in the details.

We need a complete brand identity that feels unhurried, considered and confident. Not loud. Not trend-chasing. Permanent.

Deliverables: Primary logo and mark · Wordmark · Colour palette · Typography system · Brand identity guidelines · Social presence visual language

Tone: Calm authority. The ocean at dusk, not at midday. Depth over brightness.

Avoid: Anything that feels like surf culture, anything tropical and obvious, gradients, overly organic hand-drawn marks.

Brief — Jordon Cooper

Jordon Cooper is a graphic designer and typographer based in Dubai, trained at LCC. He works across brand identity, editorial design and typography for clients in hospitality, ocean culture and conscious lifestyle. He is building toward independent practice by May 2027.

This project is the foundation of everything. Every touchpoint — business card, invoice, portfolio, email signature, website — should communicate the same thing: considered, typographically rigorous, unhurried. The work of someone who notices things others walk past.

Deliverables: Logo and personal mark · Wordmark · Colour palette · Typography system · Business cards · CV · Invoice template · Portfolio (Behance and Adobe Portfolio website) · Brand identity guidelines

Tone: Quiet confidence. Editorial intelligence. The designer is the brand — nothing should shout.

Direction: Draw from the LCC training, the typographer's eye, the ocean, Dubai light, the in-between spaces. Not a loud personal brand. A precise one.
