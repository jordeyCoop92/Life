GoPro dive footage · Fujairah · Zanzibar · Mnemba Atoll
Step 1: Apply AquaColorFix to the raw GoPro file first — handles depth colour loss.
Step 2: Export from AquaColorFix, import into Lightroom.
Step 3: Apply tone structure to match the grid:

Temp 0 · Exposure 0 · Contrast +15 · Highlights −40 · Shadows +15 · Blacks −25
Clarity +15 · Vibrance +15 · Saturation +8
Grain: 15 / 22 / 40 (same as surface presets — ties the grid together)

No Colour Grade — AquaColorFix handles colour.
HSL: Aqua Sat −15 · Blue Sat −10 · Yellow Sat −10 (apply on every underwater shot)
Radial filter on subject eye: Clarity +20, Sharpness +30.
Crop aggressively — remove blown surface highlights at top of frame.

Note: no more than one underwater shot per post sequence — if posting three times from a trip, one of those three can be underwater. More in Stories/Reels.
Per-Image Cheat Sheet
Sky too blue / saturated
HSL → Blue Sat −10 to −20 · Aqua Sat −10
Greens too neon / loud
HSL → Green Sat −20 to −30 · Green Hue +10 · Yellow Sat −15
Skin reads pink or red
HSL → Red Sat −10 to −15
Skin reads orange / burnt
HSL → Orange Sat −10 to −15 · Orange Hue +5
Image looks flat / no depth
Contrast +10 to +15 · Blacks −10
Overcast / too dark overall
Exposure +0.2 to +0.3 · Shadows +10
Highlights blown (white sky / bright deck)
Highlights −10 to −20 more · Whites −10
Night shot looks muddy / noisy
Pull Shadows back −5 to −10 · Lift Exposure +0.1 instead · Check noise reduction
Golden hour skin looks burnt
Orange Sat −10 to −15 · Red Sat −10
Foreground too bright / distracting
Linear gradient from bottom · Exposure −0.2 to −0.3

Portfolio
Tidal and Root
CLIENT: Tidal and Root
INDUSTRY: Nature, ocean, conscious travel, beach culture
BRIEF DATE: June 2026

WHO WE ARE
Tidal and Root is a brand studio built around the things that matter — the ocean, nature, craft, and genuine curiosity. We are not a lifestyle brand. We are a practice. We work with brands that share our values: slow, intentional, rooted in something real.

WHAT WE NEED
We are commissioning our founding designer to create the complete visual identity for Tidal and Root. This identity will become the face of everything we do — from client proposals to our own social presence.

DELIVERABLES
Logo system (primary, secondary, mark) · Typography system · Colour palette · Brand voice guidelines · Social presence system (grid aesthetic, post templates, story templates) · Brand guidelines document

TONE
Considered, not precious. Editorial, not corporate. Warm but not soft. Think: a well-worn field notebook, salt water, morning light on stone.

WHAT WE DO NOT WANT
Gradients. Drop shadows. Anything that looks like it was made with a template. Anything that could belong to a hundred other studios.

REFERENCES
Plant Studio UAE · Ocean conservation brands · Conscious travel editorial · The typography of well-made books

TIMELINE
Logo direction by end of June 2026. Full system by September 2026.
Own Branding — Jordon Cooper
CLIENT: Jordon Cooper
DISCIPLINE: Graphic design & typography
BRIEF DATE: June 2026

WHO YOU ARE
A graphic designer and typographer trained at LCC, currently working at O Beach Dubai. Building toward independent practice. Your work lives at the intersection of editorial precision and cultural awareness. You are not trying to be everything — you are trying to be unmistakably yourself.

WHAT IS NEEDED
A complete personal identity system that works across every professional touchpoint. This is the most important piece of work you will make for yourself. It needs to be as good as anything you would produce for a client.

DELIVERABLES
Personal logo · Typography system · Colour palette · Business card design (print-ready) · CV / portfolio document · Invoice template · Email signature · Adobe Portfolio / website · Brand guidelines (one document, everything in it)

TONE
Typographer-first. Considered. Not loud. The identity should feel like it was made by someone who cares deeply about the details — because it was.

WHAT TO AVOID
Anything generic. Anything that looks like a freelancer template. Anything that does not feel like it could only be yours.

REFERENCES
Your own taste. The best typographic identities. Studios whose work you genuinely respect.

TIMELINE
Begin after Tidal and Root logo is resolved. Complete before May 2027.
