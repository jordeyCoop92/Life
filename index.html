Feeding This Week

Tap day when fed. Only feed if ammonia below 0.25 ppm.

Protocols
Ammonia Protocol

Above 0.5 ppm → 30% change (4.8L) + Aquadene 7–8 drops + Seachem Prime 0.4ml into tank
0.25–0.5 ppm → 20% change (3.2L) + Aquadene 3–4 drops + Seachem Prime 0.4ml into tank
Below 0.25 ppm → No change, can feed small amount
Do not feed if ammonia above 0.5 ppm

Seachem Prime

0.4ml into full 16L tank. Dose directly into tank, not water change volume. Redose every 24hrs if ammonia remains high.

Aquadene

2–3 drops per 3.2L new water only. 7–8 drops for 4.8L change. Add to new water before adding to tank.

Seachem Stability

SHAKE WELL before every use. Day 1: 2ml. Days 2–7: 1ml daily. After day 7: 1ml daily until ammonia AND nitrite = 0 consistently. Once cycled: 1ml monthly.

Foster Clarks Bicarbonate

ONLY when KH below 70 AND pH below 7.0 simultaneously. 1/4 tsp dissolved slowly. NOT baking powder — Foster Clarks orange tin only.

Barking Protocol — Every Single Time

Freeze. Turn away. Zero attention. Wait for silence. The instant there is quiet: mark and reward. One person letting it slide resets everything.

Name recognition: say once, moment they look, treat. 10 reps each.
Sit Henry: treat from nose over head, bottom drops, say sit, treat.
Sit Margaret: quick 3-rep refresher, demonstrates for Henry.
Toilet Henry: outside every 45–60 mins, after every meal, nap and play.
Mouthing Henry: say ouch, freeze, redirect to toy. Every time.

Stay: sit, open palm, 2 seconds, treat. Build duration slowly.
Recall: crouch, open arms, happy voice. Never call for anything unpleasant.
Leave it: treat in closed fist, moment they back off, treat from other hand.
Place command: mat for door triggers.
